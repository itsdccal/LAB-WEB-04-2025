About Game

Grow a Garden is a popular, free-to-play farming simulator game on the Roblox platform. Players can plant seeds, cultivate crops, and harvest them to earn in-game currency to buy more seeds and land. Crops will continue to grow even when players are offline, and players can sell their harvests for "Sheckles," which can be used to expand their garden, unlock mutated plants, and craft tools.
